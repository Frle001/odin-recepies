Lasagna

Description

nice traditional meal with italian origins

salty cheese oriented meal with red meat best served hot out of the oven

Ingredients

  • cheddar
  • moyyarella
  • red meat
  • tomato sauce
  • origano
  • pasta leaves
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • flour

Steps

  1. heat the oven for 30 mins on 260 C
  2. prepare the meat
  3. put in the oven inbetween cheese for 40 minutes
  4. enjoy :]
